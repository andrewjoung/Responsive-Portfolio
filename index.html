About Me

My name is Andrew Joung. I am 23 years old and I live in Bellevue, WA. I love listening to music and in my free time I enjoy spending time outdoors. In the spring/summer I enjoy hiking, camping, golfing, playing pickle ball, and spending time with my friends. I am always down to grab a beer outside at a nice brewery. In the winter I try to spend as much time as I can up in the mountains snowboarding.

I attended the University of Washington for a while where I studied Informatics which is where my journey as a developer started. I have background in front-end development, Java, basic computer science logic including algorithms and data structures, as well as a little bit of Android development.

During this last year, I lived in Korea for 6 months teaching English which really challenged me to step out of my comfort zone. As I became ready to return back to seattle, I spent 2 weeks traveling alone in Thailand.
